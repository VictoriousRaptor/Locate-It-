[b]Helps you pin everything important, e.g. strategic resources, Leviathans and Megastructures on you map.[/b]

[h1]Locate all strategic resources and megastructures on your map![/h1]

Tired of finding living metal on your galaxy map? Busy checking your system one by one to find if there's a unfinished gateway?

[b]This mod helps you locate strategic resources and megastructures though cannot search directly due to Paradox's limitation. It can mark strategic resources or megastructures in surveyed systems on your map.[/b]

[h1]Main Features[/h1]
[list]
    [*]Pin strategic resources and megastructures on your map.
    [*]Pin unexploited strategic resources within your border.
    [*]Pin Levathans and Enclaves.
    [*]Also pin upgradable megastructures within your border.
    [*]And more...
[/list]

[h1]Usage[/h1]
[olist]
    [*]Subscribe first!
    [*][strike]Choose one between[url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/?id=1087781345]Mod Menu[/url]and[url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2458024521]Dynamic Mod Menu (3.1.*)[/url] to subscribe.[b]If you are using UI Overhaul Dynamic, please use Dynamic Mod Menu for better compatibility.[/b][/strike] These two mods are not mandatory now. You can access the mod in Edicts -> Locate It!.
    [*]If you are using [b]Mod Menu[/b], open the mod menu in the government tab (top-left corner). If you are using [b]DMM[/b] pleas press Esc, press Mod Settings and press Esc again.
    [*]Follow the guide in the popup menu. Choose what you wanna locate, then find it in your situation log, and click "Track all on map"*.
[/olist] 

[h2]How to Remove the Event Chain/Pins[/h2]

Research the top-most special project in the event chain to hide the event chain.

[h2]Compatibility[/h2]

Not achievement compatible. Compatible with game version 3.2.*. Should be compatible with all mods.
